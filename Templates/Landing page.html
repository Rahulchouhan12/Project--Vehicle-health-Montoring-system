Make your vehicles healthy...

Many of today’s vehicles include a wide range of components that perform various operations while vehicle is in use. Overtime with the usage of the vehicle, it may cause failure of the components which affects the overall performance of the vehicle. This may also cause our vehicle to use more fuel/ emit more pollution.

The domain of our proposed work is “health monitoring of vehicles”. This is usually done by Our software will help reduce air pollution by determining the right servicing required. So the vehicle emits as less smoke and pollutants as possible.

Our Project Vehicle Health Monitoring System basically would help determine the health of a particular vehicle in order to figure out if it needs more servicing, more oiling etc for it to work optimally and cause less air pollution as much as possible.
